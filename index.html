Djaa Prototype | University Project

Role(s): Director, Programmer, Game Designer

What (Genre, Platform, Synopsis)
2D action-adventure platformer for mobile (Android/ iOS) and PC (Windows/ macOS) that follows the story of Budburra and Hope during initiation (rite of passage) to become adults of the indigenous tribe.
Why (Client, Audience, Features)
Project was inspired by and built for Kerry O'Neil of Goombuckar Creations to explore, share and preserve aboriginal cultures way of life and wisdom in an engaging interactive way to young audiences (10-15 years of age) with hunting, gathering and traversal mechanics and rite of passage storyline.
How (Technology)
Technology: GameMaker Studio 2, Bitbucket/ Git, SourceTree, Trello, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Audition.

Djaa | In Pre-Production

Role(s): Director, Programmer, Game Designer
  • Documentation
  • Code
What (Genre, Platform, Synopsis)
2D action-adventure platformer for mobile (Android/ iOS) and PC (Windows/ macOS) that follows the story of Budburra during initiation (rite of passage) who is seeking to become a powerful warrior for glory to prove his honour.
Why (Client, Audience, Features)
Continuation towards full release of the university project inspired by Kerry O'Neil of Goombuckar Creations that explores, shares and preserves the aboriginal cultures way of life and wisdom in an engaging interactive way to young audiences (12-17 years of age) with flowing intuitive hunting, gathering and traversal mechanics contextualised in a rite of passage storyline.
How (Technology)
Technology: Unity, C#, Visual Studio, GitHub/ Git, Jira, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Audition, UML.
